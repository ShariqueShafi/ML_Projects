# Data Science Portfolio

Welcome to my Data Science Portfolio! This repository contains a collection of data science projects demonstrating my skills in data analysis, statistical modeling, machine learning, and text mining. 

Below you will find a list of projects, each accompanied by its problem statement, dataset, codebase (R/Python), and key findings.

## Table of Contents

1. **[Medical Insurance Charge Prediction](./Projects/Insurance_Charge_Prediction)**
   - **Topics**: Linear Regression, Multicollinearity, Heteroscedasticity.
   - **Description**: Predicting medical insurance charges using demographic and lifestyle factors.
2. **[Housing Finance & Loan Analysis](./Projects/Housing_Finance_Analysis)**
   - **Topics**: Logistic Regression, Decision Trees.
   - **Description**: Analyzing applicant data to predict loan approval and determine important applicant factors.
3. **[Total Cost to Hospital Analysis](./Projects/Hospital_Cost_Analysis)**
   - **Topics**: Multiple Regression, Data Preprocessing.
   - **Description**: Identifying key drivers of total hospital costs incurred by patients during their treatments.
4. **[Customer Segmentation & Churn Prediction](./Projects/Customer_Segmentation_Churn)**
   - **Topics**: K-Means Clustering, RFM Analysis.
   - **Description**: Segmenting customers to optimize marketing strategies and identifying at-risk customers to prevent churn.
5. **[Text Mining and Sentiment Analysis](./Projects/Sentiment_Analysis_Text_Mining)**
   - **Topics**: HuggingFace Transformers, Natural Language Processing, Word Clouds.
   - **Description**: Extracting sentiments and insights from movie reviews (IMDB, Jawan) and location reviews (Anjuna Beach, Soya).
6. **[Market Basket Analysis & Recommendation System](./Projects/Market_Basket_Recommendation)**
   - **Topics**: Apriori Algorithm, Association Rule Mining.
   - **Description**: Analyzing Point of Sale data to discover product associations and build robust recommendation systems.
