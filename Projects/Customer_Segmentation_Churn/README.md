# Customer Segmentation & Churn Prediction

## Problem Statement
This project focuses on segmenting customers based on their purchasing behavior and predicting customer churn to improve retention strategies.

## Approach
RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis was conducted. K-Means clustering was applied to segment the customers into distinct groups based on their value. Churn prediction models were also explored.

## Findings
Customers were successfully segmented into high-value, at-risk, and churned groups. The K-Means clusters helped in tailoring targeted marketing strategies.
